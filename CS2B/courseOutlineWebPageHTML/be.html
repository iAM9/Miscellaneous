Weekly Lecture Plan

Week 1 Atomic Structure, Coulomb's Law, Conductor, Insulator & SemiConductor, Basic Electronic Components, Electronic and Electrical Devices, Difference between Electrical and Electronics, Alternating & Direct Current. LAB-1: To determine the value of resistors from their EIA(Electronic Industries Association) colour code
Week 2 Voltage, Current and Resistance, Conductance, Electric Power, Energy & Work, Ohm's Law. LAB-2: Using mulit-meter measuring the resistance and voltage
Week 3 Series Circiut. Analyzing Series Circuits, Related Problems, Open, Closed and Short circuits. LAB-3 To verify Ohm's Law
Week 4 Parallel Circuits, Analyzing Parallel Circuits, Related Problems, Effect of Voltage Sourced connected in Parallel. LAB-4: Analysis of series circuits
Week 5 Voltage Divider Rule, Kirchhoff's Voltage Law, Related Problems. LAB-5: Analysis of parallel circuits
Week 6 Current Divider Rule, Kirchhoff's Current Law, Related Problems. LAB-6: Series Parallel Circuits
Week 7 Power in series and parallel circuits, introduction to series-parallel circuits, calcualting total resistance. LAB-7: Volatge Divider Circuits
Week 8 Related problems to series-parallel circuits, power in series-parallel circuits. LAB-8: Current in a parallel circuit
Week 9 Super position theorems, related problems. LAB-9: Resistance of a parallel circuit
Week 10 Semi-conductor, atomic structure, classification of matter on the basis of semiconductor theory. LAB-10: Thevenin's Theorem
Week 11 Intrinsic semiconductor, extrinsic semiconductor, doping of semiconductor, conduction in semiconductor. LAB-11: To study the characteristics of Diode curve
Week 12 n-type and p-type semiconductors, forward biasing, reverse biasing, breakdowns. LAB-12: To study the diode approximation
Week 13 pn-junction, the diode, biasing a diode, voltage-current charcteristics of a diode. LAB-13: To study the characteristics of zener diode
Week 14 Special purpose diodes, zener diodes, optical diodes, other types of diodes. LAB-14: To study common emitter connection of transistor
Week 15 Transistor structure, pnp and npn transistor, basic transisotr operation, transistors characteristics and parameters. LAB-15: To study LED drivers circuit measures the transistor switch
Week 16 Revision. LAB-16: To study LED drivers circuit measures the transistor current source and FINAL LAB
