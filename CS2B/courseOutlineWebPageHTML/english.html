                                                                               Course Objective:
                                                                                   This course has been desgined for providing detailed information for writing your own technical reports, technical expositions etc. and teaching the basics of business ethics

Weekly Lecture Plan

Week 1 Overview of technical reporting
Week 2 Use of library and information gathering, administering questionnaires
Week 3 Reviewing the gathered information.
Week 4 Technical exposition
Week 5 Topical arrangement
Week 6 Exemplification, definition, classification and division
Week 7 Casual analysis, effective exposition
Week 8 Technical narration, description and argumentation, persuasive strategy
Week 9 Organizing information and generation solution
Week 10 Brainstorming, organizing material
Week 11 Construction of the formal outline, outlining conventions
Week 12 Electronic communication
Week 13 Generation solutions
Week 14 Polishing style: paragraphs, listening sentence structure, clarity, length and order, pomposity, empty words, pompous vocabulary, document design: document structure, preamble, summaries, abstracts, table of contents, footnotes, glossaries, cross-referencing, plagiarism
Week 15 Citation and bibliography, glossaries, index, appendices, typesetting systems, creating the professional report; elements, mechanical elements and graphical elements.
Week 16 Reports: Proposals, progress reports, Leaflets, brochures, handbooks, magazines articles, research papers, feasibility reports, project reports, technical research reports, manuals and documentation, thesis. Electronic documents, Linear verses hierarchical structure documents.
